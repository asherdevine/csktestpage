About CSK

CSK is a non-profit NGO in Sale, Morocco helping children with disabilities and their families with love, compassion and commitment. CSK provides physical therapy, training, education, emotional care, practical help, and advice.

Our Mission

Empower children with disabilities and integrate them as far as possible into everyday life in Morocco.

Our Vision

CSK believes that all children are valued as individuals who grow, develop and learn. Despite their challenges, these children have the potential to live more enjoyable lives and make a contribution to society.

Our Programs

We offer weekly programs for children and young people with disabilities, their mothers, and the Kariat community. Our programs provide education, support, health, and social opportunities and are a way for kids and their families to connect and have fun out of the house.
